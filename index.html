How It Works
Three simple steps to recover your belongings

1. Report the Item

Submit a detailed report of your lost or found item including description, location, and time. Upload photos if available.

2. Search & Review

Browse through listed items or use filters to find matches. Our system also suggests possible matches automatically.

3. Verify & Reclaim

Contact the finder or admin, verify ownership through identification questions, and collect your item from the designated office.
